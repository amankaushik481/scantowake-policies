Privacy Policy

Alarm Clock - Scan To Wake

Last Updated: December 28, 2024

Summary: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Introduction

Welcome to Alarm Clock - Scan To Wake ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. The App is designed to work entirely on your device without transmitting any information to external servers.

Specifically:

  • No account required: You don't need to create an account or sign in to use the App.
  • No analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking tools.
  • No advertising: We do not display ads or share data with advertisers.
  • No data transmission: All your alarms, barcodes, and settings are stored locally on your device only.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • Alarm settings: Times, labels, and repeat schedules you configure.
  • Barcode data: Scanned barcode values and nicknames you assign to them.
  • App preferences: Your settings like sound preferences and vibration options.
  • Wake-up history: Statistics about your alarm dismissals (stored locally only).

This data is stored using your device's local storage and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning barcodes to dismiss alarms. Camera access is only used in real-time for barcode scanning and:

  • No photos or videos are captured or stored
  • No images are transmitted to any server
  • Camera is only active when you open the scanner

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to alert you when your alarm goes off. These notifications are generated and managed entirely on your device. We do not use push notifications from external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. We do not use:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for use by all ages.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secure.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete on our end. You can clear all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset All Data" option in the App's Settings
  • Uninstalling the App from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
