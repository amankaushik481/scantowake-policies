‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I stop the alarm without scanning?

That's the whole point! The app is designed to force you out of bed. Place your barcode somewhere that requires you to physically get up - like the bathroom.

What if I lose the barcode item?

Go to Settings and use "Reset All Data" to clear all barcodes and alarms. Then set up new ones.

Will the alarm work if my phone is on silent?

Yes! The alarm is designed to play even when your phone is on silent mode, just like the built-in alarm.

What types of barcodes work?

Almost all types! QR codes, UPC codes (on products), EAN codes, Code 128, and many more. Most product barcodes will work.

Does the app track my data?

No! All your data stays on your device. We don't collect any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The alarm didn't go off. What happened?

Make sure you've granted notification permissions and that "Do Not Disturb" is not blocking alarms. Also ensure the app isn't force-closed from the background.
